About GanitAnuvad

GanitAnuvad is an open-source project dedicated to translating mathematical works from various languages to Hindi. Our mission is to make mathematical knowledge accessible to a wider audience by breaking language barriers.

Get Involved

If you are passionate about mathematics and languages, we welcome you to join our community. Contribute to the translation efforts, suggest improvements, or simply spread the word about GanitAnuvad.
